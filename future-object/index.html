An Unknown Gaze

A speculative object in a future of ambient and unaccountable machines

Machines keep increasing, but we don’t know who controls them or why they move. An unknown gaze becomes part of everyday life.

Future Framing

This project begins with a 2D matrix mapping machine systems across two axes: visible and invisible, controlled and neglected.

Selected Future

I focus on a plausible future located in the invisible and neglected quadrant. Machines operate quietly in everyday spaces, while their purposes and operators remain unclear.

Speculative Object

The object represents a machine that is present but not fully legible. It suggests observation without explanation and presence without accountability.

Form & Process

Early Blender iterations focusing on form, scale, and presence rather than realism.

Reflection

This project reflects on how invisible systems become normalized, and how a constant but undefined gaze quietly reshapes everyday experience.
