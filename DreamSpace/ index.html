Dream Space

My dream space is outer space. Space is a place that humans want to reach but cannot easily access. In my imagined world, people live in space and travel through portals that connect different environments. Through these portals, they can move from space to deserts or even to other planets. This project explores the idea of space as a dream environment where travel between worlds becomes possible.

Initial Sketches

I began by sketching ideas for a dream environment in space. The sketches explore portals, floating planets, and the possibility of traveling between different worlds.

Unity Scene Development

Using Unity, I started building a space environment where objects float and portals connect different spaces. I experimented with lighting, bloom effects, and positioning of planets and portals to create a sense of depth and atmosphere.

Experiments

During the process I tested different visual effects and spatial compositions. One key element was the portal, which represents the possibility of traveling between distant environments. The portal connects outer space with other landscapes such as deserts or other planets.

Reflection

Through this project I explored the idea of space as a dream destination. Because humans cannot easily reach space, it becomes a symbolic dream environment. By introducing portals, the project imagines a world where movement between different environments becomes possible.
