Dream Space – Documentation

1. Introduction

My dream space is outer space — a place that humans cannot yet fully inhabit. Because it is physically unreachable, I construct it digitally.

2. Concept Development

I began by imagining a future where humans live in space. Portals allow travel between environments such as desert planets and oceans.

3. Spatial Structure

The space is structured as a central hub in orbit. From there, users can access different worlds through portals.

4. Interaction

Users float in zero gravity and move freely. Entering a portal transitions to another environment.

5. Technical Decisions

I chose Unity HDRP for lighting control. Initially I tested SUIMONO water system but replaced it with HDRP water for compatibility reasons.

6. Reflection

This dream space represents desire and inaccessibility. It allows me to inhabit a place I cannot physically reach.
